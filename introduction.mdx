## About Siloé Perú

Siloé Perú is a volunteer organization dedicated to accompanying and supporting hospitalized children and their families at Hospital del Niño and in vulnerable communities across Peru. Through volunteer work and the joy of hospital clowning, we transform moments of pain into instances of hope and human connection.

## Mission & Vision

<Note>
**Mission**: Accompany and support hospitalized children and their families at Hospital del Niño and vulnerable communities in Peru through volunteerism and joy, transforming moments of pain into instances of hope and human connection.
</Note>

<Info>
**Vision**: Be a movement of social transformation where volunteering is not just an action, but a way of life. A Peru where every child feels accompanied, valued, and capable of dreaming big, even in the most difficult moments.
</Info>

## Key Features

This website showcases several innovative features:

- **Multi-section Navigation**: Dynamic single-page navigation system with smooth section switching
- **3D Flip Cards**: Interactive gallery with flip animations revealing inspiring stories
- **Form Integration**: Volunteer and clown application forms integrated with FormSubmit
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach ensuring great experience on all devices
- **WhatsApp Contact**: Floating contact button for direct communication
- **Social Impact Content**: Mission-driven storytelling highlighting real transformations
